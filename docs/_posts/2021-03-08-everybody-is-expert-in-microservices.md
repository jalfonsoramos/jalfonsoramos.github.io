Microservices, that fancy word everybody is using in the industry.

Once I heard a BRM of my employer said "We have around 100 architects with expertise in microservices available to work" in front a potential (new) customer. He looked at me for a moment and with a smile in his face, he asked to the guy that said that big statement "wow! how did you achieve to have that amount of people ready to rock?"

Obviously this was a marketing lie, the BRM was not able to replied the question and just spited more fancy words trying to hide his ignorance, but it was to late, the customer just changed the conversation topic and we continued with the meeting.
